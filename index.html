What makes postcard marketing more effective than digital marketing?

Unlike digital ads that get lost in cluttered inboxes or ignored during quick social media scrolls, postcards provide a tangible presence that stays visible in customers' homes for days or weeks. Our clients see higher engagement rates, better conversion rates, and significantly higher ROI compared to their digital marketing efforts.

Who can benefit from postcard marketing campaigns?

Businesses of all sizes across various industries can benefit, including e-commerce stores, retail shops, restaurants, subscription services, beauty brands, SaaS companies, and service providers. Postcard marketing is particularly effective for customer retention, reactivation campaigns, product launches, and special promotions.

How quickly can I get started with a campaign?

You can start immediately by placing an order on our website. Within 24 hours (during business hours), our team will contact you to begin the design process.

Are there any hidden costs beyond the per-card price?

No, our pricing is all-inclusive. The rates cover design, printing, and postage stamps. There are no surprise fees or additional charges.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Yes, our minimum order starts at 500 postcards to ensure campaign effectiveness and optimal pricing.

How long will the introductory pricing be available?

Our special introductory rates are available for a limited time for new customers. We recommend locking in these rates now to maximize your savings.

Do you offer discounts for recurring campaigns?

Yes, we offer loyalty pricing for businesses that run regular postcard campaigns. Contact our sales team for details on our recurring campaign packages.

What if I don't like any of the three designs you provide?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with the initial designs, we'll work with you to understand your preferences better and create additional options (a small revision fee may apply after the initial three designs).

Can I provide my own design instead?

Absolutely! If you have your own design ready, you can submit it during the order process. Our team will review it to ensure it meets printing specifications.

What information should I include on my postcards?

Effective postcards typically include your logo, a compelling offer, clear call-to-action, contact details, and visually appealing graphics. During our consultation call, we'll discuss the best content strategy for your specific campaign goals.

How do you handle my customer data for mailing?

We take data security very seriously. All customer information is handled in compliance with data protection regulations. Your data is used solely for your campaign and is never shared with third parties.

What is the typical delivery timeframe after shipping?

Once your postcards ship, delivery typically takes 2-7 business days depending on the destination locations. We provide tracking information so you can monitor the progress of your campaign.

Can I target specific geographic areas or customer segments?

Yes, we offer precise targeting options. You can target by geographic location, demographics, or specific customer segments from your database. This targeted approach ensures your marketing dollars are spent efficiently.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we focus on domestic campaigns within India to ensure optimal delivery times and postage rates. For international campaign needs, please contact our team to discuss available options.

How can I measure the ROI of my postcard campaign?

We recommend using unique promo codes, dedicated landing page URLs, or phone extensions to track responses. During our consultation, we'll help you set up effective tracking mechanisms for your campaign.

What response rates can I expect?

Response rates vary based on your offer, audience, and industry. Our clients typically see response rates of 2-5%, significantly higher than average digital marketing response rates (0.5-1%). For customer retention campaigns, response rates often reach 8-12%.

How soon should I expect to see results?

Most campaigns begin generating responses within 3-5 days after delivery, with peak response periods in the first two weeks. For long-term offers, responses may continue for several weeks after the initial mailing.
